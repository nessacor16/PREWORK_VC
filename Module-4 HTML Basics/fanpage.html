"The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill!"

Bio

The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill is the debut solo album that is a neo sould and R&B album with some songs based in hip hop soul and reggae. The release date was August 25, 1998, by Ruffhouse Records and Columbia Records.

The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart, selling 422,624 copies in its first week, which broke a record for first-week sales by a female artist. Critics generally praised The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill for Hill's presentation of a woman's view on life and love, along with her artistic range. At the 41st Annual Grammy Awards, The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill earned 10 nominations, winning five awards, making Hill the first woman to receive that many nominations and awards in one night.
